Join WBHS Squash

Take the first step — play, compete, and grow with our team.

Why Join Us?

Being part of WBHS Squash means more than just matches. It’s about building friendships, learning discipline, and representing your school with pride. Whether you’ve never picked up a racket or you’ve been playing for years, there’s a place for you on our courts.

How to Join

Sign-ups open at the start of the school year. Visit the Sports Office or talk to one of our coaches to register your interest. Keep an eye on the school notices for trials and training times.
