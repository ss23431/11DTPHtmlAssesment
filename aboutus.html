About Squash at WBHS

Social & Beginner Squash

At Westlake Boys High School, squash is open to students of all abilities. For beginners, we provide a welcoming social league where you can enjoy a casual hit each week, improve your fitness, and build confidence in a fun environment.

Development Pathways

For players aiming to take their squash further, we run a Development Squad. These students train both independently and alongside the Premier Squad, giving them the opportunity to sharpen their skills and progress quickly to the next level.

Premier Squad

At the highest level, our Premier Squad offers structured training, strength and conditioning support, and competitive matches against top schools. Many of our Prem players have represented New Zealand, and the program continues to develop future representatives. The Prem program is one of the best programs in NZ and continues to develop to be better.

Our History at WBHS

Westlake Squash has a long and proud history, standing as the most successful secondary school squash programme in NZ. Westlake has claimed the most national titles, as well as dominating Auckland championships and league competitions. Many Westlakers have gone on to represent New Zealand on the world stage, continuing a legacy of excellence that stretches back generations. This tradition, built on strong coaching and a culture of success, has made Westlake Squash a true powerhouse in the sport.
